<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>This project visualizes hospitals that are registered with Medicare in the United States.</p>
<h2>Project Highlights</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Used SimpleMarkerSymbol to symbolize hospitals.</li>
    <li>Utilized a UniqueValueRenderer to render the different types of hospitals.</li>
    <li>Configured popups to show detailed hospital information.</li>
    <li>Filtered data on the FeatureLayerView.</li>
    <li>Shifted focus to the selected state or region after user selection.</li>
    <li>Added the Home, Legend, and ScaleBar ESRI widgets.</li>
</ul>
