<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>This project visualizes the Atlantic hurricanes that appeared in 2005.</p>
<h2>Project Highlights</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Used SimpleLineSymbol to symbolize the paths of hurricanes.</li>
    <li>Utilized a SimpleRenderer to render the hurricane paths.</li>
    <li>Used visual variables to communicate the different wind speeds of hurricanes.</li>
    <li>Configured popups to show detailed hurricane information.</li>
    <li>Customized the styles of ESRI widgets to match the overall style of the app.</li>
</ul>
