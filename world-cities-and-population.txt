<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>This project visualizes the cities in the world and their corresponding population.</p>
<h2>Project Highlights</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Used visual variables to communicate the different sizes of population.</li>
    <li>Configured popups to show detailed city and population information.</li>
    <li>Used spatial query to find the intersecting geometry of the continent and cities layers.</li>
    <li>Filtered data on the FeatureLayerView.</li>
    <li>Added the Home, Legend, ScaleBar, BasemapGallery, and Expand ESRI widgets.</li>
</ul>
